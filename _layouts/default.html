Jiangmiao Pang

Hi! I am currently a Postdoctoral Fellow at MMLab, CUHK, working with Prof. Dahua Lin. I obtained my Ph.D. degree from Zhejiang University in 2021. During my postgraduate study, I was fortunate to work with Dr. Jianping Shi at SenseTime Research (2017-2019), Prof. Fisher Yu and Prof. Trevor Darrell at UC Berkeley (2019-2020), and Prof. Dahua Lin at MMLab (2020-2021).

My research interests span computer vision, machine learning, and robotics. My long-term research goal is to build an intelligent system that can perceive and act like human beings. To this end, I am currently focusing on "perception, motion, and action in open-world robot navigation". I am also interested in video/3D/cross-modality representation learning and their applications.

Beyond research, I commit myself to open-source and act as a core contributor of OpenMMLab, where I lead/co-author/contribute to several codebases, mainly including MMDetection, MMTracking, and MMDetection3D.
